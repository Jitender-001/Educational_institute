Course Details

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program is a four-year undergraduate degree program designed to provide students with a strong foundation in engineering principles and practical skills necessary for a successful career in various technological fields. The program encompasses a comprehensive curriculum that blends theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience through laboratory work, projects, and industry internships.

Core Courses

  • Computer Programming and Problem SolvingElectrical Circuits and Networks
  • Electrical Circuits and Networks
  • Digital Electronics and Logic Design
  • Thermodynamics and Fluid Mechanics
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Software Engineering Principles
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Biomedical Engineering
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Aerospace Engineering

Industry Internship

Students are required to complete a mandatory industry internship during their third year of study, providing them with valuable real-world experience and exposure to professional engineering practices.

Final Year Project

In the final year, students undertake a capstone project where they apply their knowledge and skills to solve real-world engineering problems under the guidance of faculty mentors.
