Course Details

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

The Master of Business Administration (MBA) program is a prestigious graduate degree designed to equip students with advanced knowledge and skills in business management, leadership, and strategic decision-making. The program offers a comprehensive curriculum that integrates core business disciplines with specialized concentrations, experiential learning opportunities, and professional development activities.

Core Courses

  • Managerial Economics
  • Financial Accounting
  • Marketing Management
  • Operations Management
  • Business Analytics
  • Human Resource Management
  • Global Business Environment
  • Leadership Development
  • International Business
  • Corporate Finance

Experiential Learning

Students have the opportunity to engage in hands-on learning experiences such as case competitions, consulting projects, internships, and study abroad programs to apply classroom knowledge to real-world business challenges.

Professional Development:

IThe program offers career development workshops, networking events, and access to industry mentors to help students enhance their leadership skills, build professional networks, and explore career opportunities.

Fee Structure

  • Tuition Fee: $800 per semester
  • Registration Fee: $1500 (one-time payment)
  • Examination Fee: $300 per semester
  • Technology Fee: $50 per semester
  • Library Fee: $20 per semester
  • Career Services Fee: $100 per semester
